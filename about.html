About JaMar

Jamar gets its name from its owner, James Byrd and wife, Marilyn Byrd, his secretary. We have been working together for 30 years as a small team with amazing employees, servicing the entire valley — summer, fall, winter and spring. The Better Business Bureau has had us listed with an A+ rating for each of those years, and we strive to earn your business from the moment you call.
